Start by selecting your age/sex. Sliders adjust the scores on the fly. You can also manually input values into the boxes and click/tap calculate score. Info icons above the components pull up the score chart for the age/sex selected. Bottom left you'll find the required lap times for the time you've entered in the 1.5 mile run. If you want to get back to this page, click/tap the large info icon at the top.
